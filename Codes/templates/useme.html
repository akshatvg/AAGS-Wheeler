Learn How To Use Wheeler


1. Click on the microphone button at the bottom of the white box.


2. Speak whatever you need help with.


Some things wheeler can help you with are:

1) Recognise someone,
2) Tell you the latest news,
3) Give weather updates,
4) Read prescriptions and other documents with fine prints,
5) Navigate to a place
6) Suggest restaurants near you.


An Example Use Case

An elderly disabled person wakes up and uses Wheeler to find out the news.
A distant relative comes to meet him but he doesn't remember who it is. He uses Wheeler to recognise him.
They want to go out and hence check if the weather is suitable using Wheeler.
They use Wheeler to find out restaurants nearby and navigate there.
Imagine how much hassle was avoided because the elderly "techie" used Wheeler for his day to day activities!
He was more independent and confident than his peers who didn't use Wheeler.
